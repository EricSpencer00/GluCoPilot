GluCoPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-14

1. Overview

GluCoPilot provides AI-powered insights to help users manage their glucose and related health data. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you opt in:

- Account information: email address, username, display name.
- Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens or session tokens used to authorize access to your account.
- Health data (only if you explicitly authorize via HealthKit or Google Fit): steps, workouts, sleep records, heart rate, weight, and other metrics necessary for AI insights.
- Device and diagnostics: device model, OS version, crash logs, and anonymized analytics to improve the app.
- Third-party service credentials/tokens: OAuth tokens for services like Dexcom or MyFitnessPal if you connect them. We do not store plain-text passwords; passwords saved for third-party integrations are encrypted at rest.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

- Provide personalized AI insights and recommendations related to glucose management.
- Sync and aggregate health metrics from connected devices and services to present a unified view.
- Improve the product via aggregated, anonymized analytics.
- Send notifications and alerts related to glucose or app features (if enabled).

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- Service providers that perform operations on our behalf (hosting, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
- Healthcare providers only with your explicit consent and when you use a feature that shares data with them.
- Law enforcement or as required by law.

5. Consent and Control

- Health data: we will only access HealthKit/Google Fit after you explicitly grant permission via the operating system prompt and a clear in-app consent screen.
- You can revoke access at any time using in-app settings or the operating system's privacy controls.
- A `HealthConsent` record is stored on the server indicating what was authorized and when; this can be audited and revoked.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

- You can request deletion of your account and associated data via the in-app settings or by contacting support. We will delete your data within 30 days, subject to legal requirements.
- We retain anonymized, aggregated metrics for product improvement.

7. Security

- All data in transit is encrypted using TLS.
- Sensitive tokens are encrypted at rest.
- Access to production systems is limited and logged.

8. App Review & Apple Store Compliance

- We only request HealthKit permissions that are required for the app's core functionality.
- We provide clear Info.plist explanations for HealthKit usage.
- We do not collect or store HealthKit data without explicit user consent.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: privacy@glucopilot.app

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify users of material changes via in-app notifications and by updating the "Last updated" date above.
